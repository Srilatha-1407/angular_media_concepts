Operators

RxJS operators are a powerful feature of Angular that allow you to manipulate and transform streams of data. They can be used with observables to filter, map, reduce, and perform other operations on the data. A Pipeable Operator is a function that takes an Observable as its input and returns another Observable

An operator is a pure function that takes a observable as an input and provide the output in also in the form of an observable
